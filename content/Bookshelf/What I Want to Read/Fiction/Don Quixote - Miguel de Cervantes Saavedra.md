# Don Quixote

*by Miguel de Cervantes Saavedra*

Don Quixote is a Spanish novel by Miguel de Cervantes, first published in two parts in 1605 and 1615. It follows the adventures of Alonso Quijano, an aging nobleman who becomes so enamored with chivalric romances that he loses his sanity and decides to become a knight-errant, taking the name Don Quixote, and embarking on a quest for adventure alongside his pragmatic squire, Sancho Panza.
