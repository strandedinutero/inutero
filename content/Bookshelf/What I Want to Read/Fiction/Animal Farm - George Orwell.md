# Animal Farm

*by George Orwell*

It is one of the most famous parables in literary history. The novel is considered a parable of the history of the Soviet Union, in which the February Revolution, supported by the people, was ultimately followed by Stalin's dictatorial rule. In Eastern Germany, the book, like all of Orwell's other works, was not allowed to appear, and its possession was forbidden.
