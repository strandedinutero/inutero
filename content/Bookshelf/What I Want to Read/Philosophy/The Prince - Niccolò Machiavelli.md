# The Prince

*by Niccolò Machiavelli*

The Prince is a political treatise by Niccolò Machiavelli, written in 1513, that serves as a guide for new rulers on how to acquire and maintain power. It emphasizes that immoral actions may be necessary for political success, challenging traditional ethical views of governance.
