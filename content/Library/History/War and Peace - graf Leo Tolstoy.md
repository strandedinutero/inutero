# War and peace

*by Leo Tolstoy*

War and Peace is a historical novel by Leo Tolstoy, published between 1865 and 1869, that explores the impact of the Napoleonic Wars on Russian society through the lives of several aristocratic families. The book combines a fictional narrative with philosophical discussions, making it one of the greatest works of literature.
