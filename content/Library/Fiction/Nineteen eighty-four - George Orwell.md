# Nineteen eighty-four

*by George Orwell*

Nineteen Eighty-Four, written by George Orwell and published in 1949, is a dystopian novel that explores themes of totalitarianism, mass surveillance, and the manipulation of truth. It depicts a future society governed by a repressive regime led by Big Brother, where individual freedoms are severely restricted.
